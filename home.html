Getting There

Bristol Register Office - The Old Council House, Corn St, Bristol, BS1 1JG

Welsh Back - Welsh Back, Ferry Terminal, Bristol, BS1 4RR

Beeses Riverside Bar - Wyndham Cres, Brislington, Bristol, BS4 4SX

By Boat - Beeses is a little way out of the city centre. We have arranged a boat to take us all there and hope you will join us onboard. The journey is approx. 1 hr and very gentle. The boat will leave from Welshback at 3pm

By Car - It's a 15-30 minute journey from town if you prefer to drive/taxi. Beeses is at the bottom of a bumpy lane. There is a small car park, or you can park in Eastwood Crescent or Eastwood Road, before the bumps

Getting Home

There will be no boat home so you will need to take a taxi Beeses have a buzzer at the bar which will call a taxi direct when you want it, they tell us it takes 15 minutes

If you prefer to pre book, Vcars is a local taxi firm 0117 955 5545 or theres always Uber
